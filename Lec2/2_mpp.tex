  y_i & = & \mu + & \textstyle{{\color{mypink} \sum_k \beta_k q_{ik}}} & + \epsilon_i
               & \epsilon_i \sim \text{N}(0, \sigma^2_e) \\[24pt]
  & = & \mu + & {\color{mypink} \eta_i} & + \epsilon_i & \eta_i \sim \text{N}(0, \sigma^2_p)

$$\text{cov}(\eta_i, \eta_j) = \sigma^2_p \; (2 k_{ij})$$
